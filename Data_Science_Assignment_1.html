Loading Census Data¶

The census data is scattered among a bunch of sources. Accessing it is somewhat arcane.

  • The sf1 data set is the summary file, containing summary statistics about each region in the decennial census.
  • The vintage says we want to use the 2010 census (the most recent one).
  • vars selects some variables to download; NAME is the name of the region, P0010001 is the total population, and P0420002 is the total institutionalized (imprisoned) population. For more fun, see the full list of variables.
